# Navo

Independent, unofficial messenger for **Android, Windows, and macOS**. Sign in with your existing Telegram account — a lighter client on the Telegram network you already use.

**[Download](download.html)** · **[Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=im.navo.app)** · [GitHub](https://github.com/NavoMessenger/Navo) · [中文](zh-CN/)

> Navo is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Telegram**. Use at your own risk and follow Telegram’s Terms of Service.

## Features

- Chat list and conversations with live updates
- Stickers and animated stickers (`.tgs` / `.webm`)
- Voice notes, photos, files, and media
- Polls, checklists, and location sharing
- Contacts, profiles, and Stories-style moments
- One-to-one voice and video calls
- Light / dark themes and customizable app icons
- Privacy-minded controls such as keyword blocking
